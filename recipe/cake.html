ingredient

  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • ⅓ cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ cup semisweet chocolate chips (Optional)

Steps

  1. Step 1

    2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

  2. step 2

    3. Beat peanut butter, white sugar, and brown sugar together in a large bowl with an electric mixer until smooth. Stir egg, vanilla extract, and baking soda into peanut butter mixture; stir in chocolate chips.

  3. Step 3

    4. Drop mixture by small rounded spoonfuls onto a baking sheet about 2 inches apart.

  4. step4

    5. Bake in the preheated oven until cookies are flattened and golden, about 8 minutes
